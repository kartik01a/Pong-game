1. How to start a New Game.

* Click on New game on the screen.

* Enter the Player1 and Player2 name. Then press Start game button.

* Player1 -> Press W for moving up & Press S for moving down.

* Player2 -> Press UP key for moving up & Press DOWN key for moving down.

* Fill 'yes' or 'no' accordingly. Then press submit button.

2. How to check User Statistics.

* Click on statistics on the screen.

* Click on All Player Statistics.

* Click on Single Player Statistics.

* Enter the player name.
